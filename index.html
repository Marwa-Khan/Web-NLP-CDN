Catalogue Description:

This course introduces the key concepts of Natural Language Processing. Topics include: Overview of NLP; Language Modelling; Word Level Analysis; Syntactic Analysis; Discourse Processing; Machine Translation; Information Retrieval; Deep Learning for NLP; and Speech Recognition.
